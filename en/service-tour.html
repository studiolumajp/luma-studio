Overview

Beyond what guidebooks show.

Local air, local food, local people.

Based in Tokyo and well-travelled across Japan — from Hokkaido to Fukuoka — through aviation photography and consulting work, I know where to find the real character of a place. Tours are available nationwide, not just Tokyo and the Kanto region.

Routes are designed for atmosphere, food, and people, not just for ticking sights. Fully bilingual EN/JA, suitable for inbound travellers and Japanese learners alike.

And because I personally live with food allergies (with anaphylaxis history), gluten-free and allergy-aware dining are at the core of every tour. Restaurant pre-checks, menu reading, and emergency protocols are part of the service.
