Section 02

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

LUMA (the "Operator") considers the appropriate handling of personal information a key responsibility for delivering S.League and other services, and adopts the following policy.

1. Personal information collected

The Operator may collect the following information:

  • Name (nickname accepted)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mahjong experience and other tournament-relevant information
  • Payment information (managed by Square; the Operator does not retain card numbers)

2. Purpose of use

Collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Receiving, confirming, and communicating S.League applications
  • Billing and payment management for entry fees
  • Day-of operations and reception
  • Future event announcements (newsletters, opt-out available at any time)
  • As required by law

3. Disclosure to third parties

The Operator does not provide personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your consent
  • As required by law (e.g. requests from police or courts)
  • To service providers necessary for delivering the service (see below)

4. External services and data handling

This service operates with the following third-party tools. Please also review their respective privacy policies.

5. Photos and video

S.League events may be photographed by event staff. These photos and videos may appear on the Operator's website (studio-lumadesign.com) and SNS (Instagram, etc.). If you prefer not to be featured, please note this in the application form's notes field or contact us via the contact form.

6. Data security & retention

Collected information is protected with appropriate security measures against unauthorised access, loss, or leakage. Information no longer required for operations is disposed of appropriately after the relevant event concludes.

7. Disclosure / correction / deletion requests

To request disclosure, correction, or deletion of your personal information, please use the contact form. After identity verification, requests are processed within a reasonable period.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be revised without prior notice in line with legal updates or business changes. The revised policy takes effect upon publication on this page.

9. Contact

Substitute participant

Even when a cancellation fee would apply, you may waive it by introducing a substitute participant (someone you arrange yourself). Please share the substitute's name and contact details in advance.

Cancellation by the organiser

If the event is cancelled or rescheduled due to natural disaster, infectious disease, venue issues, or other force majeure, full refunds will be issued. If a participant changes the cancellation due to personal circumstances, the policy above applies.

Refund methods

Card payments are refunded to the original card via Square (refunds typically take 3–10 business days). PayPay and bank-transfer payments are refunded by transfer to your specified account. Note: Square's processing fee (3.25%) is non-refundable.

This cancellation policy applies to all S.League tournaments. Completing your application is taken as agreement to this policy.
