About — the founder

Sho Asano (浅野 彰)

Born in Aichi, based in Yokohama. A 9-year resident of Brisbane, Australia. Bilingual EN/JA. As LUMA's founder, Sho personally delivers all four services.

"A change of environment changes a life."

I moved to Australia at 15 and lived there for nine years. Back in Japan I worked as a consultant while building parallel practices — English education, photography, and community organising. Since 2022, I've hosted "S.League," a health-mahjong community that has grown to 22 events and 335 cumulative participants over four years.

My theme: support self-confidence with words and shared experience. Every service is built one-on-one, around you.
