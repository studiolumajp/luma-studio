Overview

Building places where people connect.

A turning point isn't a moment —
it's a place to belong.

From a childhood shaped by isolation, the thing that changed my life was finding a place where I could be myself. That experience seeded "S.League," a mahjong community I started in 2022 — now 22 events strong with 335 cumulative participants. LUMA Circle extends that playbook beyond mahjong, to social meetups, custom events, and any "place to belong" you'd like to grow with me.

Flagship Event

S.League — Four years and counting

The flagship community within LUMA Circle. "Earnest fun" is the motto.

Since 2022

Real mahjong, in a healthy way.

Started in January 2022, S.League runs 16–20 players per session in real, competitive games — without alcohol, smoking, or betting. Pros and recent beginners share the same tables. Every participant takes home a parting gift.
