Overview

Preserving moments that matter, beautifully.

A natural-light approach,
quietly cinematic.

Using the Nikon Z6III and a natural-light approach, I photograph pre-weddings, okuizome, family portraits, aviation, and landscapes. Same-day or next-day digital delivery is available — preview your photos on the spot. Follow @luma_frame.jp on Instagram.

Equipment & Style

  • 📷 Nikon Z6III + NIKKOR Z 35mm f/1.4
  • ☀️ Natural-light first; honest, unforced expressions
  • 🇯🇵 On-location across Tokyo & Yokohama
  • ⚡ Same-day or next-day digital delivery
What's Included

Each session is customised to the location, time, and people involved.

01

Pre-wedding

前撮り
