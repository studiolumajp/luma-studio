About me — Sho Asano

A change of environment
changes a life.

Based in Yokohama, bilingual EN/JA, with 9 years lived in Brisbane, Australia. I personally deliver all four LUMA services — English lessons, family photography, the S.League mahjong community, and English-guided Tokyo tours.

Profile

Sho Asano (浅野 彰)

Born in Aichi, based in Yokohama

I moved to Brisbane, Australia at the age of 15, pushed by my mother. Nine years in a place where I knew neither the language nor the culture taught me three things: how to express myself, how to let go of the fear of being wrong, and how to keep showing up for the people around me.

Back in Japan I built parallel practices — English coaching, photography, and community organising. Since 2022 I've hosted "S.League," a healthy mahjong community that has grown to 22 events and over 335 cumulative participants in four years.

My theme is simple: support self-confidence with words and shared experience. As LUMA's founder, I personally run all four service areas.
